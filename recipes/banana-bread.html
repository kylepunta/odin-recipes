Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of bread or cake that is made using mashed ripe bananas.

It is a very popular treat around the world and can be made using simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter
  • Teaspoon of baking soda
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of flours

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8 inch (10x20 cm) loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  4. Mix in the baking soda and pinch of salt. Let the mixture sit for a couple of minutes; it will start to foam.
  5. Stir the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract into the banana mixture.
  6. Gently fold in the flour until just incorporated. Be careful not to overmix, as it can make the bread tough.
  7. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick or a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the banana bread cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
